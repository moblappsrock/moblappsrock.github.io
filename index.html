Apps ~ Performance

What

Build your apps to handle your web URLs so that your users with the app get the smooth app experience you have built for them no matter where they come across your URLs.

Why

Twice the performance

Across the industry, on average, the user engagement rates are twice when users are taken to the mobile apps instead of the website.

Loyality

Users with your app are more likely to continue transacting with you in the long term. This builds loyality.

Easier integration

Information needed for transactions such as payment detials, addresses etc can be retained in the long run.

How

Android - App links

Android has built support for automatic app opens using your http URLs leveraging app links. More details on the setup are here

iOS - Universal links

iOS supports auto app opens using your http URLs by leveraging universal links. More details can be found here.
